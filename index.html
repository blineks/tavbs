About The Barbecue Co.

The Barbecue Company is a 30 year old catering, special event, and restaurant business started in Phoenix Arizona by its original owner Andrew Jepsen in 1987. The BBQ Co. operates as one of the premier catering companies in the Phoenix and outlying areas specializing in fabulous Award winning barbecue foods and many other types of international and regional cuisines. Our goal has always been to cook everything as fresh as possible using excellent quality meats, poultry and vegetables onsite when possible to ensure a hot, fresh and delicious meal.

Awards

The Barbecue Company has been nationally recognized with several awards during its 20+ years of operation. Our ribs have won awards all over the country, including the Sparks Nugget Rib Cook-Off in Reno, Nevada, the Bay City Pig Gig in Michigan, the Naperville Ribfest in Illinois, and the World Pork Expo in Des Moines, Iowa. We have also been awarded with the "National Championship" Trophy from the Sparks Nugget Rib Cook-Off, arguably the most prestigious award to win on the Barbecue circuit!


Our barbecue sauces have also won their fair share of awards. Sparks has awarded our sauce with a 1st place trophy, as did the Arizona Fiesta Bowl BBQ Classic. Chili Pepper Magazine has also repeatedly recognized The Barbecue Company for our outstanding barbecue sauces throughout the years. Most recently we were awarded the "Best of Phoenix" in the Phoenix New Times for our Sweet Sonoran Heat sauce.
